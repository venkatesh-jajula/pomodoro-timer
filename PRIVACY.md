Privacy Policy (short)

What we store

- Tasks: locally in Chrome storage (`chrome.storage.local`) so your tasks persist across sessions.
- Timer settings: preferred Pomodoro duration is stored locally.

Why we store these

- Tasks and settings are required to provide the core extension functionality (persist tasks and preferences).

Third-party access

- This extension does not send your tasks or settings to external servers.

Contact

For questions or data removal requests, open an issue on the repository or contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support contact.
